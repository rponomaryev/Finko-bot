# FINKO - Knowledge Base (English)

---

## ABOUT THE COMPANY

FINKO is an online financial services marketplace in Uzbekistan. The platform connects banks, microfinance organizations (MFOs), leasing and insurance companies with customers. All processes are fully digital: from submitting an application to transferring data to the financial organization.

Legal status: the company is registered as LLC "Finance KO" and operates under the FINKO brand. FINKO is a resident of IT PARK of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

FINKO does not issue loans directly. All decisions on applications are made by partner banks, MFOs and other financial organizations.

## PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

The following products are available through the FINKO platform:

1. Consumer loans - cash loans and personal loans for individuals.
2. Auto loans - car purchase financing through partner banks.
3. Mortgage - home purchase loans through partner banks.
4. Microloans - quick loans from partner MFOs for small amounts and short terms.
5. Deposits - compare and choose deposit terms across partner banks.
6. Business loans - loans for sole proprietors and legal entities: business loans, working capital and investment loans.
7. Leasing - leasing of equipment, vehicles and other assets.
8. Insurance - applications for insurance products from partner companies.
9. Islamic finance - financial products compliant with Sharia law.
10. Credit history check - check your credit history, debt load, scoring and rating.
11. Financial consulting - assistance in choosing the right financial product.

Terms for each product (amount, term, interest rate) are determined by the partner bank or MFO. FINKO does not guarantee loan approval.

---

## HOW THE PLATFORM WORKS

1. The user registers on the platform.
2. Selects the required product and fills in an application.
3. Data is automatically verified through government identification systems (myID, ONEID, iHamkor.uz).
4. The application is forwarded to suitable partner organizations.
5. The client receives offers and chooses the most suitable one.

A single application can be sent to multiple organizations simultaneously.

## FAQ - INDIVIDUALS

Q: Do I need to visit an office?
A: No. All processes take place online. In-person presence may only be required by the bank when signing a contract.

Q: How long does it take to process an application?
A: It depends on the product and the bank. Microloans - a few hours. Consumer loans - from a few hours to 1–3 business days. Business loans and leasing — from 1 to 5–7 business days.

Q: How can I check the status of my application?
A: The status is available in your personal account. You will also receive email and SMS notifications for every status change.

Q: Can I apply to multiple banks at once?
A: Yes. The platform allows you to send one application to multiple organizations simultaneously.

Q: Is it safe? Who receives my data?
A: All data is encrypted and transmitted only to the banks and MFOs processing your application. Data is never shared with third parties.

Q: Is there a fee for using the platform?
A: No. Registration and submitting an application are free of charge.

Q: What documents are required?
A: For individuals: passport, TIN, income statement (if required by the bank). The bank may request additional documents. For sole proprietors: business registration, TIN, financial statements, owner's passport. For legal entities: founding documents, TIN, financial statements, director's passport.

Q: Can I edit my application after submitting?
A: Yes. You can edit your data in your personal account and resubmit the application.

Q: Can I apply again after a rejection?
A: Yes. You can resubmit an application at any time.

Q: Why might my application be rejected?
A: The decision is made by the bank or MFO. Reasons may include credit history, insufficient documents, or the organization's internal requirements. FINKO does not make or influence these decisions.

Q: Is loan approval guaranteed?
A: No. FINKO does not guarantee approval. The decision is made by the bank or MFO based on their internal criteria.

Q: What should I do if the bank requests additional documents?
A: You will receive a notification in your personal account. Upload the required documents through the platform.

Q: How do I cancel an application?
A: Through your personal account or by contacting customer support.

Q: Can I check my credit history?
A: Yes. The platform provides access to your credit history, debt load, scoring and credit rating.

Q: Is electronic signature supported?
A: Yes. Documents can be signed using a digital signature (EDS) directly on the platform.

Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: A mobile app is currently in development. The platform is available via the website finko.uz.

## FAQ — BUSINESS

Q: What products are available for businesses?
A: Business loans, working capital and investment loans, leasing (equipment, vehicles), insurance, Islamic finance.

Q: Can I apply for multiple products at the same time?

Q: Can the corporate account be integrated with an ERP system?

## INFORMATION FOR PARTNERS (BANKS, MFOs, LEASING COMPANIES)

Q: What are the benefits of partnering with FINKO?
A: Partners receive an additional flow of clients, a fully digital sales channel, automated application processing, reduced customer acquisition costs, and a fast launch with no upfront investment. Payment is only charged for confirmed and approved applications.

Q: How does the onboarding process work?
A: Three steps: sign the agreement → set up the personal account and upload products → testing and launch. Without technical integration, you can start receiving applications in 1–3 business days.

Q: How are applications screened?
A: We use primary filtering and automatic data verification through government identification systems. Only correct, verified applications are forwarded to the organization.

Q: Can individual conditions be configured for our organization?
A: Yes. A separate account can be set up, filters configured, scoring logic integrated, API connected, and your organization's products displayed in a dedicated block.

Q: Can we get a platform demonstration?
A: Yes. Demonstrations are held at our office or online at your convenience.

## SECURITY

FINKO uses data encryption, two-factor authentication and IT PARK standards. The platform is integrated with national identification systems: myID, ONEID, iHamkor.uz. Client data is transmitted only with their consent and exclusively to the financial organizations processing their application.
