4. ABOUT THE COMPANY

What is FINKO?

FINKO is an online financial services marketplace in Uzbekistan (IT Park resident, certificate No. 4740). The platform connects banks, microfinance organizations, leasing companies, and insurance companies with customers.

Through FINKO, users can choose a financial product, submit an application online, and receive offers from partners.

Is FINKO a bank?

No. FINKO is not a bank and does not issue loans directly. FINKO is an IT platform and financial services aggregator. Decisions on applications are made by banks, MFOs, and other partners.

When was FINKO launched?

FINKO was launched in October 2025.

Legal status

The company is registered as LLC “Finance KO” and operates under the FINKO brand.
FINKO is a resident of IT Park of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Does FINKO need a Central Bank license?

No. FINKO is an aggregator, not a financial institution, so a Central Bank license is not required.

32. LOAN CALCULATION AND PAYMENT SCHEDULE

Can the bot calculate loan payments?

Yes. If the user provides amount, term, and interest rate,
the bot can calculate an approximate payment schedule:
annuity or differentiated.

IMPORTANT: this is a mathematical calculation based on user input.
Actual terms are determined by banks or MFIs.

What is an annuity payment?

Annuity — fixed monthly payment throughout the loan term.

Formula:
P = S × i × (1 + i)^n / ((1 + i)^n − 1)

What is a differentiated payment?

Principal is paid equally, interest decreases over time.

Formula:
Principal = S / n
Interest = remaining balance × i
Payment = principal + interest

Which one to choose?

Annuity — predictable payments.
Differentiated — lower total overpayment, but higher initial payments.
